League of Legends

A little bit about League of Legends (LoL).

League of Legends is a popular multiplayer online battle arena video game made in 2009. It has grown in popularity especially in the competative scene. The 2016 World Championship was held in Staples Center as well as other popular locations in the years following. Competative play has reached a record high of 44 million concurrent viewers.

When it comes to the game outside of competative play, it has roughly 65 million unique players monthly, 27 million unique players daily, and peaks of 8 million concurrent players.
